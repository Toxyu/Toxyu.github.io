About MycoGold Africa

Mission Statement

To empower Africa's youth with innovative, sustainable agricultural skills while promoting mental well-being, food security, and climate resilience across the continent.

Vision Statement

A hunger-free, mentally healthy, and climate-conscious Africa where empowered youth lead the charge in building sustainable communities, green economies, and a resilient future for all.

Introduction

MycoGold Africa is a youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to youth empowerment, mental wellness, and climate-conscious farming practices—with mushroom cultivation as our signature approach. Founded by a youth-focused psychologist who understands that real empowerment must address both economic opportunity and emotional well-being, our programs are not just about farming; they're about transforming mindsets, creating livelihoods, and growing purpose.

Circular Farming Model

At the heart of our work is a closed-loop, nature-based farming system designed for African communities: Mushroom Farming trains youth to grow mushrooms in urban and rural settings using locally available materials. Mushrooms are nutritious, require minimal space, and grow rapidly—offering a smart, sustainable income stream. Insect Farming (Crickets) uses mushroom waste and organic scraps, producing high-protein food and animal feed that is affordable and environmentally efficient. Worm Farming (Black Soldier Fly Larvae) turns powerful decomposers into digestible waste and turns it into rich, organic compost that revives African soils and boosts crop yields. This integrated model turns household waste into food, jobs, and soil health—perfect for communities across Africa seeking low-cost, high-impact solutions.

Our Impact Areas

Why It Matters

Too many young Africans wake up each day with nothing to do, nowhere to go, and no one who believes in them. The result? Wasted potential, rising poverty, mental illness, and hopelessness.

Climate Vision

MycoGold Africa is on the frontlines of climate-smart agriculture in Africa. We are raising a new generation of climate champions who transform organic waste into valuable agricultural products, helping African communities adapt to and combat climate change.
