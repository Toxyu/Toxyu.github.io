About MycoGold Africa

Mission Statement

To empower Africa's youth with innovative, sustainable agricultural skills while promoting mental well-being, food security, and climate resilience across the continent.

Vision Statement

A hunger-free, mentally healthy, and climate-conscious Africa where empowered youth lead the charge in building sustainable communities, green economies, and a resilient future for all.

Introduction

MycoGold Africa is a youth-led nonprofit organization dedicated to youth empowerment, mental wellness, and climate-conscious farming practices—with mushroom cultivation as our signature approach. Founded by a youth-focused psychologist, our programs transform mindsets, create livelihoods, and grow purpose.

Circular Farming Model

Our closed-loop system includes: Mushroom Farming for nutritious, sustainable income; Insect Farming (Crickets) using mushroom waste for high-protein feed; and Worm Farming (Black Soldier Fly Larvae) for organic compost to boost soil health.
